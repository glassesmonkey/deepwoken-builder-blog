The Holy Trinity of PvE Stats

Listen up, newbies! In the world of Deepwoken PvE, three stats reign supreme:

  • Fortitude: Aim for 50 to unlock Reinforce. It's like having a personal bodyguard!
  • Willpower: Pump this for Lord Commander and Grand Support. You'll be the ultimate team player!
  • Intelligence: Shoot for 40-65 to become a potion-chugging, elemental-slinging machine!

Must-Have Talents for Monster Mashing

  • Exoskeleton: Natural armor that regenerates? Yes, please!
  • To The Finish: 10% less damage when below 30% health. Clutch plays incoming!
  • Million Ton Piercer: Say goodbye to PEN cap and hello to massive damage!
  • Heretic Sutra: Pair this with Million Ton Piercer for insane damage output.

Mantras That'll Make Monsters Cry

Alright, folks, here's where the magic happens. Literally.

  • Auso Mantra: Cheat death for 40-45 seconds. It's like having an extra life!
  • Piercing Wheel: Manage your sanity, deal massive damage. Win-win!
  • Wyvern's Claw: Boost your aerial damage. Time to rain pain from above!

Pro Tips for Surviving the Grind

  1. Master the Dodge: Grab Observation or Safety Dance for extra i-frames. Can't hit what you can't touch!
  2. Gear Up Smart: Balance DVM and HP. You want to be tough, not a glass cannon.
  3. Weapon Wisdom: Evan's Handex or Hydro Tubros. Choose your poison based on your build.
  4. Speed is Key: Use Glacial L and Metal Ball for quick key grabs. Time is loot, people!

Building Your PvE God

Ready to put it all together? Here's a sample build to get you started:

  • 50 Fortitude (Reinforce is your new best friend)
  • 90 Intelligence (Elemental mayhem, baby!)
  • 55 Charisma (For those sweet, sweet support skills)
  • 25 Agility (Just enough for some key talents)
