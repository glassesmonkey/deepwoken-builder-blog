  • Top Pick: Gran Sudaruska (75 ICE, 30 HVY - it's like hitting people with a weaponized glacier)

Elemental Weapon Mechanics: The Science of Magical Mayhem

Time for some big brain energy (but don't worry, we'll keep it fun):

  • Scaling: Often scales with both a weapon stat (like MED or HVY) AND an elemental stat
  • Special Effects: Each element has unique status effects (burn, freeze, shock, etc.)
  • Elemental Damage: Bypass some physical defenses and hit those sweet elemental weaknesses
  • Coolness Factor: Immeasurable. Scientists are still trying to quantify it

Pro Strats for Elemental Weapon Users

  • Combo Like a Boss: Mix elemental effects with physical attacks for maximum pain

  • Look Flashy: Half the battle is won by looking cooler than your opponent
