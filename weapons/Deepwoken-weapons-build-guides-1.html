Deepwoken Weapons 101: Your Ticket to Becoming a Bonafide Badass

Published on August 5, 2023 | By Alex

Yo, Deepwoken newbies and weapon enthusiasts! 👋 Ready to transform from a clumsy noob into a legendary butt-kicker? Buckle up, because we're about to embark on an epic journey through the wild and wacky world of Deepwoken weapons!

Deepwoken Weapons 101: Know Your Arsenal

In Deepwoken, weapons are like Pokémon - gotta catch 'em all! But instead of cute little monsters, you're collecting tools of destruction. Let's break down the types:

  • Light Weapons: These speedy gonzales include daggers, fists, and rapiers. They're faster than your mom when she hears the ice cream truck, but hit like a grumpy kitten.
  • Medium Weapons: The Goldilocks of weapons - not too fast, not too slow. Swords, spears, and clubs fall into this category. They're like that friend who's good at everything, annoyingly balanced.
  • Heavy Weapons: The big kahunas! Greataxes, greatswords, and hammers are here to party. They're slower than a sloth on vacation, but hit harder than reality after college.
  • Elemental Weapons: These fancy pants come with special powers. It's like your weapon ate a Mario power-up!
  • Offhand Weapons: The Robin to your Batman. Shields, parry daggers, and even guns can be used offhand. Because two weapons are always better than one, right?

Weapon Stats: Decoding the Matrix

Each weapon in Deepwoken comes with its own set of stats. It's like a dating profile, but for weapons:

  • Damage: How much ouchie it does. Higher is better, unless you're into tickle fights.
  • Penetration: How good it is at ignoring armor. It's like your weapon has x-ray vision!
  • Scaling: How much stronger it gets as you level up. It's the weapon equivalent of hitting the gym.
  • Weight: How much posture damage it does. Basically, how good it is at making enemies do the wobble dance.
  • Range: How far you can bonk from. Because sometimes, you want to hit things from waaaay over there.
  • Swing Speed: How fast you can swing. Some weapons move like a caffeinated squirrel, others like a sloth doing tai chi.

Weapon Mechanics: The Science of Bonking

Now, let's get into the nitty-gritty of how these bad boys work:

  • Attack Speed: Calculated as (1/SwingSpeed) ÷ 2. Don't worry, you won't need to do math in the middle of a fight!
  • DPS (Damage Per Second): Weapon Damage × Swingspeed × 2. For when you really need to know how efficiently you're destroying things.
  • Stances: Some weapons can be wielded one-handed or two-handed. It's like choosing between eating a burger with one hand or two - it changes the experience!
  • Special Effects: Some weapons come with cool extras like bleed damage or elemental effects. Because sometimes, just hitting things isn't enough - you want to set them on fire too!

Pro Strategies for Weapon Selection

Alright, future weapon masters, here's how to choose your perfect battle buddy:

  1. Know Your Playstyle: Are you a hit-and-run ninja or a "stand there and take it" warrior? Choose a weapon that complements your style. Don't be a bull in a china shop if you're built like a twig!
  2. Check the Requirements: Some weapons need you to be buff (high strength) or smart (high intelligence). Don't try to wield a giant hammer if you can barely lift a spoon.
  3. Experiment: Don't be afraid to try new things. You might discover your inner axe-wielding maniac or realize you're secretly a rapier prodigy!
  4. Look for Synergies: Some weapons work great with certain talents or mantras. It's like finding the perfect cheese for your wine, but deadlier.
  5. Consider Your Build: Planning on being a tanky titan? Maybe avoid the dainty daggers. Want to be a speedy assassin? Perhaps the giant hammer isn't for you (unless you're into irony).

Fun Weapon Facts to Impress Your Friends

  • The weapon with the highest base damage is the Hivelord's Hubris. It's like holding a truck in your hands!
  • Changing stances doesn't affect swing speed. So, no need to do the weapon cha-cha!
  • The Aranea is the first weapon with an unobtainable stat requirement. It's like the popular kid in school - hard to get!
  • You can pass down soulbound weapons for 60 echoes each. It's like weapon inheritance, but cooler.
  • The weapon with the highest swing speed is the Divine Dagger from Crazy Slots. It's faster than your excuses when you're late for work!

Conclusion: Wield Your Weapon with Pride!
