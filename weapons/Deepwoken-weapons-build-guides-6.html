Offhand Mechanics: The Nitty-Gritty

Time for some big brain energy (don't worry, we'll keep it fun):

  • Posture: Shields increase your posture, making you harder to stagger
  • Dual-Wielding: Offhand weapons match the swing speed of your main weapon
  • Blocking: Shields can block attacks, but watch out for guard breaks!
  • Stat Requirements: Most offhand gear needs some investment in FTD or LHT

Pro Strats for Offhand Masters

  • Mix and Match: Try different main weapon and offhand combos
  • Know When to Block: Timing is everything with shields
  • Use Your Environment: Shields are great for navigating hazards
  • Surprise Attacks: Use offhand weapons to catch enemies off-guard

Fun Offhand Facts

  • The Targe is the starter shield - it's like the tutorial of the shield world
  • Some talents and mantras require shields. It's like they're playing favorites!
  • Offhand guns do reduced damage when paired with non-light weapons. Balancing is hard, okay?

Conclusion: Embrace the Power of the Other Hand!

Offhand weapons and shields in Deepwoken aren't just accessories, they're game-changers. Whether you're blocking blows with a shield or sneaking in extra hits with an offhand weapon, remember: in Deepwoken, the left hand knows exactly what the right hand is doing!
