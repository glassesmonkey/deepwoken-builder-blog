Damage Calculation: When Math Meets Mayhem

Alright, let's break down how Deepwoken decides just how much "ouchie" your weapon does:

The Basic Formula

For weapons with one scaled attribute (excluding bleed and modifiers), here's the magic formula: 

Damage = 0.00075 x {BaseDamage x AttributeScaling x AttributeLevel x [1 + (Proficiency x 0.065)]} + BaseDamage

Let's break this down for the non-mathlete:

  • BaseDamage: The weapon's starting damage. Think of it as the weapon's "personality".
  • AttributeScaling: How well the weapon vibes with your stats.
  • AttributeLevel: Your level in the relevant attribute. Gym time pays off!
  • Proficiency: Your skill with the weapon. Practice makes perfect!

Damage Multipliers: Because Basic Math Was Too Easy

But wait, there's more! When you factor in damage multipliers: 

MultipliedDamage = WeaponDamage x (1 + DamageModifiers%/100)

Bleed Damage: Making Your Enemies Leak

For our vampire wannabes, here's how bleed damage works: 

BleedDamage = WeaponDamage x 1.3

Or if you want to get fancy with multipliers:

BleedMultipliedDamage = WeaponDamage x (1 + [DamageModifiers% + 30%]/100)

Attack Speed: The Art of Swoosh

Ever wondered why some weapons feel like they're moving through molasses? Let's talk attack speed!

The Speed Formula

Here's how to calculate how long it takes for your weapon to go swoosh: 

Attack Speed (in seconds) = (1 / SwingSpeed) / 2

In human speak:

  • A weapon with 0.5x swing speed takes about 1 second to swing
  • A weapon with 1.0x swing speed takes about 0.5 seconds to swing

DPS: For the Spreadsheet Lovers

Want to know your Damage Per Second? Here's a simple formula (excluding bleed, endlag, and ping): 

DPS = Weapon Damage x SwingSpeed x 2

Pro Tips for Weapon Mechanic Mastery

  • Balancing Act: High damage often means lower speed. Find your sweet spot!
  • Stat Synergy: Match your weapon's scaling to your highest stats for maximum pain
  • Proficiency Matters: Don't neglect your weapon skills. Every point counts!
  • Bleed is Your Friend: For sustained damage, bleed effects can be a game-changer
  • Speed Kills: Sometimes, a faster weapon with lower damage can outperform a slow heavy-hitter

Fun Facts to Impress Your Friends

  • Changing stances doesn't affect swing speed. It's all about that base (speed)!
  • The weapon with the highest base damage? Hivelord's Hubris and Divine Greatsword, both at a whopping 30!
  • The fastest swing speed belongs to the Divine Dagger at 1.3x. It's basically teleporting at this point.
