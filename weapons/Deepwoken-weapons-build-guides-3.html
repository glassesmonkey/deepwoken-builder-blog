Medium Weapons in Deepwoken: Unlocking the Power of Versatility

Why Medium Weapons Rock in Deepwoken

Medium weapons are like that friend who's good at everything - they've got a bit of everything going for them:

  • Balanced Speed and Power: Hit hard without feeling like you're swinging a tree
  • Versatility: One-handed? Two-handed? Why not both?
  • Range: Keep enemies at a comfortable "you can't hit me but I can hit you" distance
  • Variety: From stabby spears to bonk-tastic clubs, there's something for everyone

The Fantastic Four of Medium Weapons

1. Swords: The All-Rounders

Ah, swords - the weapon of choice for heroes and edgy anime protagonists everywhere.

  • Top Pick: Serpent's Edge (75 MED, 15% penetration - slice through armor like butter!)
  • Pro Tip: Master the art of switching between one-handed and two-handed stances for maximum flexibility

2. Spears: The Pokey Bois

For when you want to keep your enemies at arm's length... plus a few more feet.

  • Top Pick: Rifle Spear (80 MED, 25% penetration - it's a spear AND a gun, what's not to love?)
  • Pro Tip: Use that sweet, sweet range to poke enemies before they can even think about hitting you

3. Clubs: The Bonk Masters

Sometimes, you just want to hit things really hard. Enter the club.

  • Top Pick: Pernach (75 MED, 30% penetration - it's like a mace, but angrier)
  • Pro Tip: Great for dealing with heavily armored foes - armor doesn't mean much when you're being bonked!

4. Rifles: The Shooty-Stabby Hybrid

For those who can't decide between melee and ranged combat. Why not both?

  • Top Pick: Iron Blunderbuss (75 MED, 25% penetration - when you absolutely, positively need to shoot AND stab someone)
  • Pro Tip: Use the gun blast to create space, then close in with melee attacks. It's like a deadly dance!

Medium Weapon Mechanics: The Nitty-Gritty

Let's get a bit technical (but not too technical, we're not writing a thesis here):

  • Damage: Generally higher than light weapons, lower than heavy weapons (duh)
  • Swing Speed: The Goldilocks zone - not too fast, not too slow
  • Scaling: Often scales well with MED stat, making them great for balanced builds
  • Stance Switching: Many medium weapons can switch between one-handed and two-handed stances

Pro Strats for Medium Weapon Users

  • Master Stance Switching: Be unpredictable! Switch it up to confuse your enemies
  • Use Your Range: Medium weapons often have better range than light weapons - use it!
  • Mix It Up: Combine light attacks, heavy attacks, and special moves for deadly combos
  • Don't Forget Your Offhand: When one-handing, use shields or offhand weapons for extra oomph

Conclusion: Embrace the Balance!

Medium weapons in Deepwoken are all about versatility and balance. They're the Swiss Army knives of the weapon world - ready for any situation. Whether you're slashing with swords, poking with spears, bonking with clubs, or blasting with rifles, remember: with medium weapons, you're always prepared!
